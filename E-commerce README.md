# 🚀 NovaLink Smart Living: Data-Driven Market Entry Analysis

This project demonstrates a full-cycle data science approach to launching a Smart Home startup in the US market. By analyzing over 100k e-commerce records and simulating a year of business operations, I established a scalable growth strategy backed by unit economics.

---

## 📊 Project Highlights
* **Strategic Market Selection:** Identified **Smart Home Electronics** as the optimal niche with a **28.5% Net Margin**.
* **Marketing Efficiency:** Evaluated acquisition channels, finding **Email Marketing** as the top performer with a **30.4x LTV:CAC ratio**.
* **Geographic Insights:** Leveraged spatial analysis to identify **South Dakota (SD)** and **Vermont (VT)** as high-value regional markets.
* **Predictive Modeling:** Developed a 30-day sales forecast to optimize inventory and cash flow.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Data Processing:** `Python`, `Pandas`, `NumPy`.
* **APIs & Data Sourcing:** `Kaggle API`, `Google Trends (Pytrends)`, `Faker`.
* **Visualization:** `Seaborn`, `Matplotlib`.
* **Analysis Framework:** Star Schema (Relational Modeling), Time-Series Forecasting, Unit Economics (LTV/CAC).
